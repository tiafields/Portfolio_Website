👋 I am Tia Fields

UX / UI Designer & Developer

What I do

UX / UI Design

My enthusiasm for UX/UI design complements my strengths in attention to detail, user empathy, and prioritizing user needs. I specialize in crafting personas, designing user journeys, creating mood boards, and developing wireframes, and prototypes utilizing tools such as Adobe and Figma.

Front End Development

By combining my knowledge in UX/UI and Front-End Development, I transform ideas into reality using languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript. I translate conceptual designs into responsive and visually captivating websites. My expertise extends to optimizing user interfaces, ensuring smooth navigation, and creating meaningful user experiences. Proficient in the latest front-end frameworks like React and Bootstrap, I excel in transforming static designs into dynamic web applications. This allows me to create interactive and user-friendly digital experiences that resonate with both clients and end-users alike.

Back End Development

As a backend developer, my skills include Python, Django, JavaScript, Express, and React, allowing me to construct efficient and scalable web applications. Proficient in server-side scripting and API development, I specialize in ensuring seamless data management and optimal performance. Leveraging strong skills in Tkinter and Node, I enhance user interfaces to provide a cohesive and intuitive user experience. Experienced in version control using Git, I actively contribute to the development of robust backend systems. With a toolset that includes Visual Studio Code and GitHub, I bring a comprehensive approach to building dynamic and interactive web applications.

Who I am

Designer and Developer based out of Louisville, Ky

Hello, I'm Tia, a UX/UI Designer and Web Developer with a unique background in Technical Theatre. Having served as a Theatrical Electrician, Programmer, and Designer for several years, I've honed skills in collaboration, effective communication, punctuality, and the importance of being a team player.

With the onset of the pandemic, I seamlessly transitioned to UX/UI and Web Development, utilizing my innate sense of design. Creating projects that come to life and benefit users is i ncredibly rewarding. Drawing from years in collaborative arts, I bring effective troubleshooting skills, efficiency, patience, and empathy to design in order to build intuitive applications that enhance users' technological experiences.

Outside of wireframing, prototyping, and coding, I live an active lifestyle. I enjoy traveling, running, climbing, and yoga. However, I also partake in occasional sedentary activities from time to time, such as reading, sewing, and my ultimate guilty pleasure: video gaming

I am fueled by the transformative impact of creating applications that not only benefit people but also enhance their lives. I look forward to the continued pursuit of meaningful innovation and user-centric design in this exciting journey.
